Scoring methodology — universal across all three sources. NDR and BCA provide explicit position and benchmark weights. Active weight = position % − benchmark %. That active weight determines the signal. BlackRock BII provides directional signals which are mapped directly.
Formula: Active > +5pp → Heavy OW (+2) +2 to +5pp → OW (+1) ±2pp → Market Weight (0) −2 to −5pp → UW (−1) < −5pp → Heavy UW (−2)
Consensus: Average score of all firms that cover the asset. Score >1.5 = Strong Bullish · 0.5–1.5 = Bullish · ±0.5 = Neutral · −0.5 to −1.5 = Bearish · <−1.5 = Strong Bearish. Assets not covered by a firm are excluded from that firm's contribution to consensus.
