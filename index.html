Mathematical scoring schema — identical for every source, every asset, every month. Two modes depending on what the source provides:
Mode A — Weighted (position & benchmark available)
active = position% − benchmark%
active > +5.0pp → Heavy OW (+2)
active > +2.0pp → Overweight (+1)
active ≥ −2.0pp → Market Weight (0)
active ≥ −5.0pp → Underweight (−1)
active < −5.0pp → Heavy UW (−2)

NDR: stocks/bonds/cash + regional bonds
BCA: all assets (position & benchmark given)
BLK: ETF weight vs BLK's own stated benchmark (42% MSCI ACWI + 18% MSCI USA + 38% Bloomberg Universal + 2% T-Bill)
Mode B — Directional (view label only)
OVER / BULLISH → Overweight (+1)
MARKET / NEUTRAL → Market Weight (0)
UNDER / BEARISH → Underweight (−1)

NDR: equity cap/style, sectors, gold,
  oil, EM debt, HY (no weights given)
BCA: macro overlays (directional only)
BLK: n/a (always Mode A)
